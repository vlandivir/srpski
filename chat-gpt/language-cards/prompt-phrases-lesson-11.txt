Useful phrases - Korisne fraze Lesson 11 - Lekcija 11
This is $40. = Ovo je 40 dolara.
It is too expensive for me. = Preskupo je za mene.
Is there anything cheaper? = Postoji li nešto jeftinije?
Is this on sale? = Da li je ovo na rasprodaji?
There is a big discount in that store. = Veliki je popust u toj radnji.
All right. I will take it. = U redu. Uzeću ga / je.
* something masculine - uzeću ga - something feminine - uzeću je
Would you like this sweater? Yes, I’ll take it. = Da li biste želeli ovaj džemper? Da, uzeću ga.
Will you buy this shirt? Yes, I’ll buy it. = Da li ćeš kupiti ovu majicu? Da, kupiću je.
Where is the cash register? = Gde je kasa?
Here is your change. = Izvolite kusur.
May I pay with a credit card? = Da li mogu da platim karticom?
You can pay over there. = Možete platiti tamo.
The check, please. = Račun, molim vas.
Please give me the receit. = Molim Vas dajte mi priznanicu.
Thank you for your good service. = Hvala Vam na dobroj usluzi.
Please, come again. = Molim Vas, dođite opet.
What would you like to eat? = Šta želiš da jedeš?
Anything to drink? = Nešto za popiti?
A small glass of wine, please. = Malu čašu vina, molim Vas.
Are you ready to order? = Da li ste spremni da naručite?
I would like some spaghetti and dessert. = Ja bih želeo špagete i dezert.
Do you want some fruit? = Da li hoćeš neko voće?
Yes, I want an orange. = Da, hoću narandžu.
What would you like for dessert? = Šta biste želeli za dezert?
I will have some cheesecake. = Ja ću čizkejk.
This is delicious. = Ovo je ukusno.
Here is your food. = Izvolite Vašu hranu.
Waiter, can I get the check, please? = Konobar, mogu li dobiti račun, molim Vas?
Are you paying by check or credit card? = Da li plaćate čekom ili karticom?
Can I pay with Apple Pay? = Mogu li da platim Epl-pejom?
Sorry, we only take cash. = Izvinite, primamo samo keš.
I would like to make an appointment with Mrs. Smith. = Želeo bih da zakažem sastanak sa gospođom Smit.
She is free on Monday and Tuesday. = Slobodna je u ponedeljak i utorak.
Can I see her before Tuesday? = Mogu li da je vidim pre utorka?
At what time? = U koje vreme?
From 3 to 3:30. = Od tri do pola četiri.
Let me see. She has 30 minutes on Monday afternoon. = Da vidim. Ona ima trideset minuta u ponedeljak popodne.
If something happens, please call me. = Ako se nešto desi, molim Vas pozovite me.
I can come any time except Sunday. = Mogu da dođem bilo kada osim u nedelju.
Please call me before you come. = Molim te pozovi me pre nego što dođeš.
I have an interview this afternoon. = Imam intervju danas popodne.
He phoned to cancel the meeting. = Telefonirao je da otkaže sastanak.
Make an appointment through my secretary. = Zakažite termin preko moje sekretarice.
I have to change my appointment from Tuesday to Wednesday. = Moram da promenim termin od utorka na sredu.
Do you have any discomfort? = Da li imate neku nelagodu?
I think I have a cold. = Mislim da sam se prehladio.
You need an injection. = Treba Vam injekcija.
You need a surgery. = Treba Vam operacija.
Is it serious? = Da li je ozbiljno?
Do I need to be hospitalized? = Da li treba da budem hospitalizovan?
